1.  Open the day's puzzle.  For example, Dec 1 2022 is at
    https://adventofcode.com/2022/day/1
    
2.  Highlight all the narrative, copy-paste to file 'story.txt' in the
    day's' folder.

3.  Highlight the example data and save to 'example.txt'

4.  Right-click "get your puzzle input" and "Save link As.." to file
    'input.txt' in the day's folder.
    
5.  Use file 'template.py' as a starting point and enjoy.
